Adventure Awaits

Trail Riding

Kick up some dirt and unleash your wild side on a thrilling four-wheeler expedition through Vancouver’s stunning backcountry. Conquer rugged trails, forested paths, and breathtaking mountain views—all from the seat of your own ATV. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a first-time explorer, this off-road adventure promises adrenaline, nature, and memories that last a lifetime.

Frozen Expedition

Experience the thrill of adventure like never before aboard Canada’s iconic Ice Explorer, as you journey across the ancient Columbia Icefield in the heart of British Columbia. Surrounded by towering glaciers and pristine alpine views, this unforgettable tour lets you step right onto the ice and witness nature’s frozen masterpiece up close. A true bucket-list experience for every explorer at heart!
